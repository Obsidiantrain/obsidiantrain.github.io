Mountain Hikes

The hikes around these mountains are to die for! You can either go on foot or even on tram for those who might have trouble moving around! If you never seen 200 different types of tropical bird this is the place to do it!

Sea Side Journeys

Why not take a trip out to the lake and get a little water on your face! The lakes around our mountains are filled with amazing water life that are able to be fed.
You will be able to see:

  1. Lots of Fish!
  2. Beavers!
  3. And Elk!

You can even rent out one of our boats or even bring your own! We even have a special big boat for exclusive parties!
